To create a launcher straight to MP to not cause issues, right click JB_Launcher_s.exe in root, and make a shortcut. In the shortcut box it should say something like: "C:\Program Files (x86)\Activision\Quantum of Solace(TM)\JB_Launcher_s.exe"

Simply add -multiplayer, like this: "C:\Program Files (x86)\Activision\Quantum of Solace(TM)\JB_Launcher_s.exe" -multiplayer
